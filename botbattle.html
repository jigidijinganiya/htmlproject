BotBattle

AI Programming Challenge • Team Competition (2-3 members) • ₹599 + taxes

Welcome to BotBattle, the ultimate AI programming showdown where your code will face off against others in strategic battles!

In this competition, teams will design and program AI bots to compete in various game scenarios. Your bot's intelligence, strategy, and adaptability will be tested against other participants' creations in an epic tournament-style competition.

This event features:

  • Multiple battle arenas with different rule sets
  • Real-time visualization of bot battles
  • Workshops and practice sessions before the main event
  • Judging by AI experts from leading tech companies
  • Exciting prizes for the top-performing bots

Supported languages: Python, Java, JavaScript. Each team will submit their bot code which will be executed in a controlled sandbox environment.

The event will be hosted by Dr. Alan Turington, renowned AI researcher and game theory expert, who will provide commentary and analysis throughout the tournament.
