Treasure Trails

Adventure Challenge • Teams of 3-5 • ₹299 + taxes

Treasure Trails is an exciting adventure challenge where teams solve puzzles, follow clues, and navigate through a series of checkpoints to find the hidden treasure.

Features:

  • Interactive puzzles and riddles at each checkpoint
  • Physical and mental challenges combined
  • Use of maps, compasses, and GPS coordinates
  • Bonus challenges for extra points

Teams will need to demonstrate problem-solving skills, teamwork, and quick thinking to decipher clues and navigate the course. The first team to reach the final treasure wins!
