App Quest

App Development Challenge • Teams of 2-4 • ₹299 + taxes

App Quest is the ultimate app-building showdown! Participants will brainstorm, design, and prototype an innovative mobile app under time constraints, responding to real-world themes revealed at the start of the event.

Event Highlights:

  • Theme revealed on the spot
  • Wireframing & prototyping
  • Live Q&A with mentors
  • Pitching session to expert panel

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned coder, App Quest lets your ideas take flight!
