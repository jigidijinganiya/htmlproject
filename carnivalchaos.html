Welcome to Carnival Chaos, a wild and colorful carnival game tournament where players compete in classic fairground challenges for ultimate bragging rights!

Step right up to test your skills in ring toss, balloon darts, and more, in a vibrant festival atmosphere. Whether you’re aiming solo or rallying a team, Carnival Chaos is your ticket to high-energy fun and epic prizes.

Event highlights:

  • Classic carnival games with competitive twists
  • Festive booths with lights, music, and cotton candy
  • Skill workshops to master game techniques
  • Exclusive carnival tokens and plushie prizes
  • Awards for top scorers and best team spirit

Games: Ring Toss, Balloon Pop, Skee-Ball, Whac-A-Mole. Open to solo players or teams of 2-4, with family-friendly options available.

Hosted by Rosie "Ringleader" Quinn, a carnival enthusiast and lively MC, who’ll keep the crowd buzzing with gameshow-style energy.
